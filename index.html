About Us

Our long-standing excellence in sport is now matched in theatre, music, film, and tech. We’re a fully catered college with standard and en-suite accommodation and outstanding facilities to help you pursue whatever is important and meaningful to you.

The social hub of the college is our bar, The Stag’s Head; the creative hub is our Performing Arts center; our sport and leisure facilities are varied, and we have a library, resource center, and study space to support the academic side of your life.
